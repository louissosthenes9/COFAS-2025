About COFAS

Our Mission

The COSTECH Field Application System (COFAS) was developed to streamline the application process for students seeking field placements at the Tanzania Commission for Science and Technology. We aim to nurture future leaders in science and technology by providing real-world experience in research and innovation.

Our Vision

COFAS envisions empowering students through hands-on experience in Tanzania's leading technology institutions, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed to drive the nation's development agenda in science and technology.

Why Choose COFAS?

COSTECH offers a unique opportunity for students to work with top researchers and experts in various fields of science and technology. Through COFAS, students are placed in a variety of settings that align with their academic background and career aspirations, providing valuable hands-on experience that prepares them for the future.
